# Pay After Placement

- https://youtu.be/62qemXJfqy0?si=nxtkrqONdi3_0mQD

## Description
This video explains the concept of Pay After Placement (PAP), where students pay course fees only after securing a job. It covers how the model works, its benefits, and important considerations before enrolling.

## Key Points
- No upfront payment is required
- Payment is made after getting placed
- Terms and conditions may vary by program
